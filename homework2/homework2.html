Queen Elisabeth Park

Inside the city, this 52-hectare park is a great place to walk, admire flowers and small aninals.

It has a spectacular view of the city and it is the home of the Bloedel Conservatory. Easy access by public transportation.

Joffre lakes

Stunning views of turquoise lakes. If you like hiking, this is an amazing tour, 2,5 hours from downtown.

In this park there are 3 lakes at the mountain. To reach the first one, the trail is very easy, but for the others you will need to be in a good health condition. The effort worths the beautiful view!

More information: www.joffrelakes.ca
